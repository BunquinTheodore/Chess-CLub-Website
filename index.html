Chess Community We Offer

Dive into the Batangas State University Alangilan Chess Club, where passion and strategy converge, forging friendships on the iconic 64 squares. Join us in this vibrant chess community!

Club Membership

Join our community of chess enthusiasts! As a club member, you'll have access to regular tournaments, chess strategy workshops, and the opportunity to connect with fellow chess lovers. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, our club provides a supportive environment for players of all levels.

Weekly Chess Events

Participate in our weekly chess events, where you can challenge fellow members, engage in friendly matches, and enhance your strategic skills. It's a great opportunity to foster camaraderie and learn from each other in a relaxed and enjoyable setting.

Chess Lessons

For those looking to improve their game, we offer chess lessons conducted by experienced coaches. Whether you're aiming to enhance your opening repertoire, master strategic concepts, or sharpen your endgame skills, our lessons cater to players with diverse goals.
